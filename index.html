WHAT IS PLANT BOX?

PlantBox is a smart gardening system designed for effortless indoor growing. It automatically controls light, moisture, to give your plants the perfect environment. No green thumb needed — just plug it in and enjoy the harvest!

HOW DOES IT WORK?

Insert a seed capsule, fill the water tank, and let PlantBox handle the rest. Using smart sensors, it regulates light and hydration while monitoring plant health through a connected app. Watch your plants grow — effortlessly.
